Instruction

"Little Square" is a two player turn based game.
The two player called "Red" and "Blue" take turns to color the Board with 1x1 square or 2x2 square.
The player who can't make a turn lose the game.
You can click on obstacle button on the beginning of the game to make the game for challenging.
To toggle between 1x1 and 2x2 square, you can click the toggle button or press '1' for 1x1 square or press '2' for 2x2 square

Happy playing!
